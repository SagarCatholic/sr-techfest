About The TECHFEST-2K19

  • TECHFEST'2k19 - Festival of Technology is an annual TECHFEST.

  • It includes various events of Robotics, Technical Quiz, Scavanger / Coding / Decoding, LAN Gaming, Working Model Exhibition, Non Technical Fun Games and many more aimed to enhance Technical Knowledge of budding Engineers.

  • Last year, TECHFEST'2K18 attracted around 10000 students from different Engineering Colleges of the state. Prizes more than 2 lacs were distributed to The Winners of various events.

  • This year, TECHFEST2K19 is confident of having Footfalls of more than 150000 students from different Engineering Colleges of Gujarat.'
